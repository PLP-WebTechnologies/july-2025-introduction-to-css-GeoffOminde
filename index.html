Overview and definitions

A quick primer on what we mean by AI, regulation, and ethics—so we’re speaking the same language.

What is AI?

Artificial intelligence refers to systems that learn patterns and make predictions or decisions. It spans recommendation engines, chat systems, computer vision, and more.

Regulation vs. self-governance

Regulation is public oversight via law or standards. Self-governance includes company policies, model cards, audits, and red-teaming—useful, but not a substitute for accountability.

Ethics in practice

  • Fairness: Minimize biased outcomes across groups.
  • Privacy: Respect consent and data minimization.
  • Transparency: Explain how systems work and are used.
  • Safety: Reduce harm and enable recourse.
